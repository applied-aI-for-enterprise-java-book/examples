Dragon Fun is a steel sit-down roller coaster.
After boarding the ride, riders climb to the top of the lift hill 45 m (148 ft).
The track is 1,285 m (4,216 ft), and the ride's top speed is over 104 km/h (65 mph). The ride's duration is 1 minute and 45 seconds

The minimum height to enter the Dragon Fun ride (height restriction) is 140 cm.
